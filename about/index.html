Message from the President

It gives me great pride to welcome you to Mstar Holding.

Since our inception in 1998, we have pursued a clear vision: to build a business that grows with purpose and creates lasting value for the people and communities we serve. What began with a focus on real estate, food, and defense has developed into a diversified conglomerate with a growing presence across Asia, Europe, and Africa.

For nearly three decades, our journey has been shaped by ambition, resilience, innovation, and the trust of our partners. Every milestone we have achieved reflects long-term thinking, disciplined execution, and a commitment to delivering meaningful results for our customers, stakeholders, employees, and communities.

At Mstar Holding, we believe success is measured by more than financial performance. It is measured by the positive impact we create, the opportunities we open, and the confidence we inspire. This belief continues to guide our decisions as we expand into new industries and international markets.

In Thailand, we are proud to contribute to national development by supporting projects that strengthen security, improve access to affordable living, and promote sustainable economic growth. Through our investments and partnerships, we aim to deliver solutions that enhance quality of life while supporting long-term resilience.

Beyond our home country, our global footprint continues to grow through strategic collaborations with governments, corporations, and institutional partners across the United States, the United Kingdom, Germany, France, Ukraine, Malaysia, China, Iraq, Nigeria, South Africa, and other markets. These relationships allow us to exchange expertise, foster innovation, and create opportunities that extend beyond borders.

We recognize that today’s world is deeply interconnected. Challenges such as urbanization, food security, infrastructure development, technological transformation, and geopolitical change require organizations that can think globally while acting responsibly at the local level. Mstar Holding embraces this responsibility by building partnerships based on mutual respect, transparency, and shared success.

Diversification has always been one of our greatest strengths. Rather than relying on a single industry, we have built a portfolio that includes real estate development, food and agriculture, defense solutions, technology, investment, logistics, hospitality, and emerging sectors that will shape the future economy. This approach gives us resilience, agility, and the ability to identify opportunities that create sustainable value.

Innovation remains central to everything we do. As industries evolve through digital transformation and advanced technologies, we continue to invest in modern solutions that improve efficiency, strengthen competitiveness, and support growth. Whether through artificial intelligence, smart infrastructure, advanced manufacturing, or next-generation systems, we are committed to staying at the forefront of progress while upholding the highest standards of integrity and governance.

Equally important is our commitment to people. Our employees are the foundation of Mstar Holding, and we are dedicated to fostering a culture where leadership, collaboration, diversity, and continuous learning can thrive. We also value our partners, whose trust and shared commitment have helped us build lasting relationships with governments, financial institutions, private enterprises, investors, and local communities.

Corporate responsibility is a core principle of our business. We believe companies have a duty to contribute positively to society while protecting the environment for future generations. Across our operations, we strive to uphold responsible practices, support community development, promote sustainability, and maintain ethical standards in every market where we operate.

Looking ahead, our ambition is to become a globally respected conglomerate recognized for excellence, integrity, and meaningful impact. We will continue to grow, innovate, and build partnerships that strengthen communities and create long-term prosperity.

To our clients, partners, investors, employees, and friends, thank you for your continued trust and support. We are honored to share this journey with you.

Jakapun Viwatkurkul Founder & President Mstar Holding

About Mstar Holding

Mstar Holding was founded in 1989 under the name JSJ Enterprise Co., Ltd., with the launch of its proprietary fashion brand Susi Dee in Los Angeles, California. In 1992, operations expanded to Thailand under Point Image (Thailand) Co., Ltd., followed by the establishment of an American Buying Office in Los Angeles in 1998.

In 2015, all operating entities were consolidated under a single holding structure, Mstar Holding Inc., strengthening the group’s global management, strategy, and operational efficiency.

Today, Mstar operates as a global buying and sourcing office, providing procurement and supply-chain solutions for leading U.S. retail chains such as Walmart, Kroger, Costco, Target, Kohl’s, and Macy’s. The company also collaborates with renowned international brands including Polo Ralph Lauren, Disney, and Warner Bros.

Our American Buying Service employs more than 200 professionals worldwide, delivering end-to-end sourcing, quality control, compliance, and logistics solutions to clients across global markets.

Recognizing the decline of traditional brick-and-mortar retail and the rapid rise of e-commerce, Mstar expanded its capabilities in 2010 to support online businesses. These services include digital marketing, product formulation, fulfillment, and logistics, designed to help international online retailers reduce freight costs while increasing sales performance in the U.S. market.

In addition, Mstar has developed strategic programs to help overseas manufacturers build their own brands and sell directly to major U.S. retail chains—eliminating intermediaries. This direct-to-retail approach allows manufacturers to increase margins, reduce shipping costs, and compete more effectively with U.S. importers through faster response times and superior service.

Innovation is in our DNA

Mstar operates as a diversified holding company, integrating all business units under one corporate structure. The group is active across a wide range of industries, including technology, real estate, trading, entertainment, finance, electronics, medical, and food, with products and services distributed to more than 30 countries worldwide.

Research and development are central to Mstar’s long-term strategy. We continuously invest in innovation to remain competitive in fast-evolving markets, with a strong focus on artificial intelligence, software development, web technologies, and advanced defense-related technologies.

Innovation is not just a function at Mstar—it is the foundation of how we grow, compete, and create long-term value globally.
