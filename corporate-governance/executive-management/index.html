Executive Management

Our executive leadership team drives disciplined execution, operational excellence, and strategic growth while upholding the highest standards of integrity and organizational stewardship.

President / Founder

Jakapun Viwatkurkul

Jakapun Viwatkurkul is the founder and President of Mstar Holding. With more than 30 years of experience across real estate, logistics, food, finance, technology, entertainment, and international trade, he has led the expansion of Mstar Holding into multiple sectors and long-term business opportunities.

In 1989, Jakapun started Susi Dee, a buying office that served major department stores, chain stores, and importers across the United States and Canada, assisting them with production orders for apparel, food, and consumer goods throughout Asia. He later expanded his business ventures through BabyTales Inc., further strengthening his experience in international trade, consumer products, and cross-border business development. In 1995, he founded a finance company, and in 2001 he opened a wholesale mortgage lending company and real estate development company. By the age of 36, his businesses had reached an estimated $100 million to $350 million in revenue.

Jakapun later created Mstar Inc. as a holding company and became Chief Executive Officer of a technology research and development company focused on high-end technology and military-related projects. His major ventures include Mstar Holding, which owns and supports businesses across multiple industries, including Mstar Property, American Buying Service, FoodOnlines.com, Mstar Technology, and other group companies.

In addition, Jakapun manages a food agency and distribution company together with fulfillment operations across the United States, Europe, and Southeast Asia, including Thailand. He is also involved in online food businesses such as FoodOnlines.com, AmericanBuyingService.com, and BBQYummy.com. His interest in entertainment later led to the launch of Boogoo Music & Art Festival, an LGBT music festival, Wolf Howl Music Festival in the United States and Asia, and Mstar Airsoft as part of the Group's entertainment and lifestyle sector.

Jakapun continues to serve as a driving force behind the Group's long-term growth, strategic development, and cross-sector expansion.

Vice President / CEO of Mstar Defense

Pasit Viwatkurkul

Pasit Viwatkurkul is the Vice President of Mstar Holding and brings a strong entrepreneurial background shaped by a long history in the startup ecosystem. His successful exit from his previous venture, Music Freelancer, established his reputation as a capable and forward-thinking business leader. He has worked across multiple sectors including real estate, technology, and now defense. As the CEO of Mstar Defense, Pasit has driven the company's rapid growth into a $2.8 billion enterprise, firmly positioning Mstar Holding within the global defense industry.
