Principles for Responsible Business

Mstar Holding expects its business activity to be guided by integrity, professionalism, respect, responsible judgment, and compliance with applicable laws and market expectations.

Act with integrity and accountability.

Respect people, partners, customers, and communities.

Protect company information and business trust.
